Our Story

Comfort doesn't have to be ordinary.

Born from a simple frustration — why are all cushions the same boring rectangle? We started sketching shapes inspired by architecture, geology, and the curves of the human body. Each Contours piece is sewn in small batches at a family-run workshop in Porto, using fabrics chosen for how they feel in ten years, not just today.

Materials

Fabrics that age with grace.

Corduroy, bouclé, cotton velvet, and heavyweight linen — all sourced from European mills. Contrast piping on every edge. Removable covers with hidden zippers. Filled with recycled polyester fibre that holds its shape, year after year.
